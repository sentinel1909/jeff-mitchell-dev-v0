Homeworld 3

2024-05-13 Day 1

Played through the basic intro tutorial. Feels familiar, yet new and fresh. The graphics are simply stunning. I'm very happy to see there are difficulty levels and the ability to pause during the game. I finished the original Homeworld, but never finished Homeworld 2, which I found enormously difficult and unbalanced. We'll see how well I do with Homeworld 3.


I fear multibeam frigates though...I worry that they are a unit without a counter, resulting in unbalanced gameplay. Time will tell.


Homeworld 3

2024-05-14 Day 2

Finished the tutorial. Many old concepts, some new ones to practice. Struggling a little with issuing orders to units and having them actually follow through. The game has a new turret unit type. You can launch them and have them attach to surfaces (asteroids, etc) to guard an area. I love the art and the graphics, such a leap over the original games. So far I'm running smoothly on my NVIDIA 2070 Super graphics card.


Started the campaign missions on Easy mode. I tend to get frustrated easily (unfortunately), so I just want to play through the story first and not get my butt kicked. The first mission is really an extension of the tutorial, guide our mothership, the Khar-Kushan through a series of trials to work out the kinks and get it space worthy. The banter amongst the crew is fun. At the end, Higaaran high command informs us that, with nothing by a half-ready ship and a plucky can-do attitude, we have to go out into the galazy to figure out who or what has weaponized hyperspace, with sinister plans to destroy the known universe. I've never understood the notion of destroying the known universe...starting over is overrated, but I digress...


I saved at the beginning of the 2nd mission. Our computer/human commander, Imogen S'Jet, after a slightly odd trip through hyperspace, has guided us to a place where we can finish the Khar-Kushan by adding a production module. This is just a little bit important because you can't build a fleet with out it. More to come...


Homeworld 3

2024-05-15 Day 3

Finished the first mission and managed to get the Khar-Kushan it's production module. It was a little touch and go, and I'm not sure I left the mission with the right fleet balance. Can always replay.


Made it to Mission 2, which involves repairing a hyperspace gate which will allow a jump directly to Noctua-27, our main objective. Noticing a trend from the original games. Regardless of how much the chatter is, delay the mission objectives and build. This is a numbers game and I can see already it's essential to have more than the opponent. I think there may still be timers and triggers, but hopefully by not going after them right away, they'll be delayed. Having some difficulty with navigation and movement, more practice is needed. I also need to remember that you can pause.
